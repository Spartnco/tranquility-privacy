Privacy Policy — Tranquille

Effective date: January 3, 2026
Developer: Ambrelys Studios
Contact: ambrelysstudios@gmail.com

Summary

Tranquille is a peace-of-mind mobile application designed to help users keep visual proof of everyday checks. The application works entirely locally on your device and does not track users.

Data Collection

Tranquille does not collect personal data.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No remote servers storing user data

Camera Usage

The app uses the camera only to let you take proof photos. Photos are stored locally in the app’s private storage and are never uploaded.

Local Storage & Retention

Proof photos and timestamps are stored locally on your device. You may delete them manually or allow automatic deletion based on your selected retention period.

Payments

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties.

Security

Data is stored locally in private app storage. No cloud storage is used.

Children’s Privacy

Tranquille does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
